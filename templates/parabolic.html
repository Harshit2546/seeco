Parabolic Springs

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-performance Parabolic Leaf Springs designed for various vehicle applications including E-rickshaws, Tata trucks, Bharat Benz vehicles, and Tata Ace mono vehicles. Our springs are engineered for superior load capacity, ride comfort, and durability under harsh conditions.

These springs are manufactured using advanced technology and high-quality materials to ensure optimal performance and longevity. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we strive to deliver products that meet the highest industry standards.
