Your Trusted Manufacturer for Durable & Customized Leaf Springs

As a leading name in the Indian suspension component industry, our company is committed to delivering high-quality leaf spring solutions for all types of vehicles—from compact cars and electric rickshaws to heavy-duty trucks and commercial trailers. With decades of experience and a skilled engineering team, we not only manufacture standard leaf spring assemblies but also specialize in crafting **customized leaf springs** tailored to the specific load, size, and ride quality needs of our clients. Whether you're a fleet operator, OEM supplier, or aftermarket distributor, we work closely with you to ensure perfect compatibility and performance.

Our product range includes: Parabolic Leaf Springs for superior ride comfort and durability, Conventional Multi-Leaf Springs for rugged load-bearing use, E-Rickshaw Leaf Spring Assemblies for lightweight electric vehicles, Truck and Trailer Springs designed for long-haul stability, and Jeep and Passenger Car Springs optimized for balanced suspension in everyday use.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality manufacturing. Every product is crafted using advanced processes such as **automated camber forming, heat treatment, shot peening, bushing pressing, and powder coating** to meet or exceed industry standards like IS:1135. We use high-grade raw materials like EN 45A, SUP9, and 60Si2Mn to ensure strength, flexibility, and long fatigue life. Custom solutions are offered in terms of spring length, leaf count, eye type (open/closed), bushing material (rubber/bronze/PU), and finish (black enamel, powder-coated, or zinc-plated).

Our leaf springs are compatible with a wide range of vehicle models in India, including: Tata Ace, Tata 407, Tata Yodha, BharatBenz 2823/2828, Ashok Leyland Dost, Mahindra Bolero, Commander 750, Jeep 150, Marshall, FC 360D, Maximo, Gio, Load King, Scorpio, Tavera, Maruti 800, Ambassador, and more. For electric mobility, we also supply fully assembled **E-Rickshaw spring sets (commonly 24-inch)** ready for direct fitment with U-bolts, clamps, and bushings.

Backed by reliable service, timely delivery, and client-centric production runs, we are proud to be a preferred choice for leaf spring solutions in India’s automotive supply chain. Whether you require a standard part or a custom batch, we are here to support your suspension needs with precision, performance, and long-term reliability.
