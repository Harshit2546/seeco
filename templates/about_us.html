Seeco Industries

Who We Are

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company , based in Mohan Nagar and are trusted manufacturer of leaf springs for trucks and other commercial vehicles. Established in the year 2000, we have grown consistently under the leadership of our Chairman, Mr. Prashant Jain.

With over 25 years of experience, we have become a reliable name in the commercial vehicle components industry, both in India and abroad.

Our Journey

From a small-scale unit to a well-recognized industry name, Seeco has come a long way. Our continuous focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has helped us grow steadily over the years.

We are proud to say that we have built long-term relationships with our customers, suppliers, and business partners based on trust, performance, and consistency.

Manufacturing Excellence

  • Strength and durability
  • Accurate fit and finish
  • High performance in tough conditions

We are equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and modern production facilities that help us manufacture leaf springs as per exact client requirements. Our dedicated team of skilled professionals and engineers ensures that every product passes strict quality checks before delivery.

Reputation in the Market

  • Consistently delivering top-quality products
  • Fulfilling bulk orders on time
  • Offering fair pricing and dependable service

Our name is respected among commercial vehicle manufacturers, dealers, and exporters, and we are proud to be considered a preferred supplier in the leaf spring industry.

Global Reach

In recent years, we have expanded our presence in international markets, and today we proudly export leaf springs to several countries. Our goal is to continue growing globally while maintaining the same values that made us successful in India.

Our Commitment

Certifications

ISO 9001:2015

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Seeco Industries is proud to be ISO 9001:2015 certified for the design and manufacturing of leaf springs. This internationally recognized certification reflects our strong commitment to quality management, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement.

Our ISO 9001:2015 compliance ensures that every leaf spring we produce meets the highest standards of performance, safety, and reliability—making us a trusted partner in the automotive and industrial sectors.

Registered Importer & Exporter – IEC Certified

Registered Importer & Exporter – IEC Certified

Seeco Industries is a government-registered entity with a valid Importer Exporter Code (IEC) issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), India. This certification enables us to legally export our high-quality leaf springs worldwide and import materials essential for our manufacturing excellence.

Our IEC registration reflects our commitment to global trade compliance and positions us as a reliable partner for international clients and distributors.

GST Certificate

GST Registered Business – Transparent & Compliant

Seeco Industries is fully GST registered under the Goods and Services Tax regime of India, ensuring complete tax compliance and transparency in all our business transactions.

Our GST registration allows us to serve customers across India efficiently, while maintaining clear and lawful invoicing for both B2B and B2C operations.

For all purchases and supplies, our GSTIN is available on official invoices and can be verified through the government portal.
